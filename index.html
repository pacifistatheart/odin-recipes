My First Recipe!

This is a simple and delicious recipe for homemade spaghetti carbonara. It's quick to make and perfect for a weeknight dinner. Enjoy the creamy sauce made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper, all tossed with perfectly cooked spaghetti.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 4 oz pancetta, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
